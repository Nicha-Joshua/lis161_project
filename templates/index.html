WHAT WE OFFER

The Academic Medic offers various services that ensures quality experience for all the students and tutees.

Elementary-Based Tutoring

Students from kindergarten to sixth grade undergo fundamental changes in elementary school as they are introduced to new subjects and learning experiences. Here at Academic Medic, we provide them with the instruments they need to meet these challenges and give them the motivation, confidence and skills to succeed both inside and outside the school.

High School-Based Tutoring

The Academic Medic is committed to training high school students to be college-ready; From Languages, Literature, Communication, Mathematics, Philosophy, Natural Sciences, and Social Sciences, your study aid is at your service.

College-Based Tutoring

With the pandemic limiting face-to-face interaction, universities turned to new online peer tutoring pathways. However, students are still coping and are facing difficulties in learning. In Academic Medic, we help students to achieve their goals and be the best version of themselves.
